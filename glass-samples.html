3rd revision of the glass treatment.
• Card opacity: 0.06-0.12 (was 0.20 in v2, 0.45 in v1) — now the actual sharp objects (rings, triangle, square, dot) are clearly visible through the glass, not just blob colors.
• Backdrop blur: 14px (was 24-30px) — less blur so shapes keep their edges through the glass.
• Bg blob blur: 25-30px (was 50-60px) — softer bg gives the shapes more presence.
• Objects now have glow shadows for more "neon" feel.
