outer glow: ::after with linear-gradient + blur(8px)
Card 04 (from scratch) uses the exact pattern from your Apple reference: • A very transparent dark glass base so the bg is clearly visible • A thin bright border (the strongest "this is glass" signal) • An explicit top sheen (linear-gradient inside the card) for the "light hitting the top edge" look • A subtle outer glow for premium feel • 8px backdrop blur (Apple's actual Liquid Glass uses 4-12px)

The 5 bg shapes (rings, triangle, square, dot, wordmark) are visible through the card with their edges mostly intact — the visibility you asked for.
