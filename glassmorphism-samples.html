Glassmorphism card options

Four approaches for the case study cards. Each card sits in a stage with color blobs behind the glass. Pick the one that feels right.

A — Dark glass on light bg

The card is a dark glass window on a light cream/bone stage. Bright color blobs (lavender, peach, sky) sit behind the glass and get blurred through it. Card text is white. The dark card pops against the light bg.

What changes between options:
• A & B — light site, light/dark glass variants. The blobs are pastel (lavender, peach, sky).
• C — full dark mode, chrome-like blobs. Most "premium" feel but loses the colorful brand personality.
• D — dark base, colorful blobs (pink, amber, sky). Best of both — the dark "real glass" look from the reference, but with the DreamGully brand colors.
